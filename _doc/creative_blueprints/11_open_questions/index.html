Open questions

Affects architecture & scope
Q1

🪪 Attribution without accounts

Sharing is public but V1 has no accounts. How are sightings attributed, and how does a collection survive a reinstall?

Q2

💎 Rarity needs a backend

Tiers depend on global spotting counts, so even an accountless V1 needs a shared store and a breed-frequency source.

Q3

📚 Canonical breed list

Completeness needs a fixed master list. Source and count TBD, and must match what the AI can return.

Q4

🤖 Breed ID model

Provider, coverage, on-device vs API, latency against the sub-30s target, plus mixed-breed and no-dog photos.

Q5

🛡️ Content moderation

A public map and feed broadcast user photos. Minimum moderation and reporting needed for V1.

Q6

📡 Offline capture

Behavior offline at capture: queue the snap and identify or share later, or require connectivity.

Q7

📍 Map privacy

Exact GPS vs coarsened location on the public map, to avoid revealing homes. Flagged for the handoff so it is not discovered late.
