Texture & motion

Tactile, not flat: riso/halftone grain, die-cut sticker shadows, stamped-ink metadata. No glass, no gradient text.

Two motion tiers: loud payoffs stay rare (capture reveal, stamp-to-dex). Quiet craft is everywhere (press scale, staggered rise-in, rolling counts). Ease-out, no bounce. Respects prefers-reduced-motion.
