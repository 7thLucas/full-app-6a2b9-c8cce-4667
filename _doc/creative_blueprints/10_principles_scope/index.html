How We Build It · Pl. 10

Principles & V1 scope

Strategic principles
1

Snap → Share is the heartbeat

The entire UX optimises around the two-step loop. Everything else serves it.

2

Speed of capture is critical

See-a-dog to shared in under 30 seconds. Friction kills the habit.

3

Every interaction is a Pokédex entry

Satisfying, complete, collectible.

4

The collection is the emotional payoff

It must feel like a trophy case of every dog ever spotted.

5

Breed ID confidence matters

Show the AI's confidence score, and let the user override.

Scope boundaries · V1
✓

One encounter = photo + share + AI breed ID + location + notes = one entry.

✓

Snap → Share is the primary V1 loop. Sharing is first-class, not a future add-on.

✓

Audience is global: every dog lover, not a closed personal diary.

✓

Breed accuracy shown with a confidence score so the user can correct it.

✕

No accounts or multi-device sync in V1. Sharing is lightweight, not a full social network.

Tone: joyful, clean, personal. Warm, playful, slightly nerdy. The “got it!” moment.
