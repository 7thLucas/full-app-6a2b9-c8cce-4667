The Problem

Dogs everywhere — but no way to remember them.

You see fascinating dogs every day — at the park, on the street, at the café — but there's no quick way to identify the breed on the spot, record where you saw it, or build a personal catalogue of your encounters. Those moments just fade.

The Solution

A Pokédex for dogs — in your pocket.

Dogdex lets you photograph any dog you encounter, instantly identifies the breed with AI, tags the GPS location, and adds the entry to your growing personal collection. Every dog you've ever spotted — catalogued, located, and memorable forever.
